Features

Why OpenMind?

Built for the people, by the people. No paywalls, no gatekeepers, no compromises.

Free Forever

No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no "premium" tiers. Access powerful AI tools completely free, forever. Funded by the community, for the community.

Open Source

Every line of code is open and auditable. Fork it, improve it, contribute to it. Transparency builds trust — and we believe in radical transparency.

Decentralized

Built on Base chain for fast, low-cost transactions. No single point of failure. No single entity controls the network. Truly permissionless AI.

Community Governed

$MIND holders vote on protocol upgrades, feature development, and fund allocation. Your voice matters. Governance is on-chain and transparent.

Roadmap

The Journey

Building the future of open, decentralized AI — one milestone at a time.

Q2 2025

Foundation

Smart contract development & audit. $MIND token deployment on Base. Website launch. Community building on Telegram & X. Initial documentation.

Q3 2025

Launch & Growth

DEX listing on BaseSwap / Uniswap. Airdrop campaign to early supporters. Governance portal launch. First AI model integration. Community grant program begins.

Expansion
