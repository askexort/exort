# Email Drafting Guide

## Structure
1. **Subject** — clear, specific, actionable
2. **Greeting** — appropriate for context
3. **Opening** — state purpose in first line
4. **Body** — details, context, evidence
5. **Ask** — clear call to action
6. **Closing** — professional sign-off

## Tone
- Professional but human
- Concise — respect their time
- Specific — avoid ambiguity
- Actionable — what do you need from them?

## Common Types
- **Request**: "Could you... by [date]?"
- **Update**: "Here's the status on..."
- **Follow-up**: "Following up on..."
- **Introduction**: "I'm reaching out because..."
- **Thank you**: "Thanks for... it helped with..."

## Don'ts
- Walls of text
- Vague subjects ("Quick question")
- Multiple asks in one email
- Passive-aggressive tone
- Reply-all when unnecessary
