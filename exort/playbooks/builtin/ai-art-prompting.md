# AI Art Prompting Guide

## Structure
**Subject** + **Style** + **Details** + **Lighting** + **Mood** + **Camera**

## Style Keywords
- Photorealistic, cinematic, anime, watercolor, oil painting, pixel art
- 8k, ultra detailed, sharp focus, depth of field, bokeh
- Studio lighting, golden hour, neon, dramatic, soft light

## Negative Prompts
ugly, blurry, low quality, distorted, deformed, bad anatomy, watermark

## Example
"A majestic wolf standing on a snow-covered mountain peak at sunset, photorealistic, 8k, dramatic lighting, golden hour, cinematic composition"
