# Creative Writing Guide

## Process
1. **Brainstorm** — generate ideas freely
2. **Outline** — structure the narrative
3. **Draft** — write without self-editing
4. **Revise** — improve clarity and flow
5. **Polish** — fix grammar, style, word choice

## Techniques
- Show, don't tell
- Use active voice
- Vary sentence length
- Strong verbs over adverbs
- Specific details over vague descriptions
- Dialogue reveals character

## Story Structure
- Hook (grab attention)
- Setup (characters, world)
- Conflict (tension, stakes)
- Climax (peak moment)
- Resolution (satisfying ending)

## Style Tips
- Read your work aloud
- Cut unnecessary words
- One idea per paragraph
- Consistent point of view
- Natural dialogue
